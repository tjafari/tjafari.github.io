Tom Jafari is a Product Manager based in NYC

about

I love working with smart, enthusiastic people to discover things that are valuable, usable, and feasible. My background includes 10 years of product management, tech strategy, and process improvement experience, which I believe gives me a broad range of skills to support teams to deliver great customer experiences.

Most recently, I've been a product manager at Amazon for the past four years, and I'm currently Director of Product at comiXology, Amazon's premiere digital comic, graphic novel, and manga reading experience. I've got an MBA from Berkeley and degrees in Economics and Applied Math from the University of Washington.
